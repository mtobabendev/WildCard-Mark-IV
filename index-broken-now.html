About Us

We build web experiences that feel sharper, cleaner, and more alive than the usual brochure site. WildCard DEV is where strong frontend polish, practical development, and a little theatrical flair all work together.

The goal is simple: make your business feel memorable the moment someone lands on the page. Blue and violet establish the system. Penny’s pink arrives only where it matters. That contrast becomes the signature.

What we're doing

  • Web Design

    Modern interface direction, responsive layouts, and premium brand presentation designed to feel intentional instead of templated.

  • Web Development

    Self-contained builds, polished interactions, and production-minded code that stays readable instead of turning into a patchwork mess.

  • Play Store

    Android-ready product surfaces and companion pages that make mobile launches feel cohesive across web and app touchpoints.

  • Mobile Apps

    Phone-first and watch-aware thinking from the start, with UX patterns that still feel good once the screen gets tiny.

  • Apple Store

    Companion experiences and product framing for iPhone-first customers who expect the visual polish to match the platform.

  • Graphic Design

    The flashy one on purpose. Brand marks, banners, key art, and high-impact service visuals designed to sell the work before anyone reads the paragraph.

Built for polished brands, but not only polished backgrounds.

WildCard DEV can absolutely build the premium-tech stuff. We can also help serious people who are building the next chapter of their life — including trade businesses, field-service companies, and founders coming out of prison who need a site that looks legitimate on day one.
